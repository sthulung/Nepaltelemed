About us

We are a group of volunteer doctors/nurses in Nepal providing free telemedicine to Nepal for free. Telemedicine is a way of providing health care by bringing a doctor together remotely with his or her patient. A doctor at home can help the remote user who may be in an emergency or need treatment. Telemedicine takes place via telephone, video chat, instant messaging and other ways of electronic communications including the internet.

    It can significantly reduce the pressure on hospitals, patients do not need to travel long distance to hospital, thus reducing their mobility constraints and costs.

    Telemed is a medical practice whereby a patient's illness or symptoms are observed by a doctor without physically examining the patient.

    Nearly half of all medical appointments are unnecessary. Telemedicine can lower exposure to covid and traffic to already full hospitals.
